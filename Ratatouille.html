About the Movie

Ratatouille is a 2007 American animated comedy film produced by Pixar and released by Walt Disney Pictures. The film follows the story of Remy, a rat who dreams of becoming a chef and tries to achieve his goal by forming an alliance with a Parisian restaurant's garbage boy.
