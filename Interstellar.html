About the Movie

In a future where Earth is dying due to climate disasters and crop failures, a former pilot named Cooper is recruited for a secret NASA mission to find a new home for humanity. He must leave his family behind and travel through a wormhole near Saturn to reach another galaxy. Along with a team of scientists, Cooper explores distant planets that might support life. As they journey through space, they face extreme dangers, experience time dilation near a black hole, and grapple with emotional and ethical decisions. The story is both a grand space adventure and an emotional drama about the bonds between parents and children — especially the relationship between Cooper and his daughter Murph.
